CONSTANT EP

Hydrae







"CONSTANT" is an self-released debut EP with hard drone sounds, disorienting melodies, and vocal samples wrapped around 172BPM boundary. That was a pretty nice experience to push out something that undercooked, especially when mostly working with analogue synthesizers, some really simple samples, field recordings and arranging everything on the spot.






Production & Mix: Mikołaj Połonowicz

Additional Production & Equipment: Dawid Eichler Cover

Art: Mikołaj Połonowicz & Adrian Smoliński
